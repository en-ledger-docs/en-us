Getting Started with Ledger

Welcome to Ledger, the most secure way to manage your cryptocurrency assets. Here’s a quick guide to help you get started:

  • Connect Your Device: Begin by connecting your Ledger hardware wallet to your computer or mobile device using the provided USB cable.
  • Download Ledger Live: Install the Ledger Live app to your device. It’s the companion app to your hardware wallet, allowing you to easily manage your digital assets.
  • Security Setup: When setting up your Ledger device, make sure to store your recovery phrase in a safe place. This is essential for recovering your assets if your device is lost or damaged. Always ensure you’re in control of your private keys.
  • Install Cryptocurrency Apps: Use Ledger Live to install apps for various cryptocurrencies you wish to manage. The Ledger Live app supports a wide range of coins, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many others.
  • Transactions and Portfolio Management: Once set up, you can start sending and receiving crypto, checking balances, and accessing decentralized applications (dApps) for added functionality.

For a detailed step-by-step guide and troubleshooting tips, visit Ledger’s Official Start Page.
